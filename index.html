Some stories end.

...

And then,

somehow,

you keep returning to them.

Do you want to go back?

Then tell me—

where did you
first find them?

Memories blur with time.

What was this
one called?

Ah.

You do remember.

You already know what happens.

Stay anyway.

Somewhere between the first note
and the last goodbye,

they stayed.

You remember the sound of it.

The sound arrives
before the scene does.

being forgotten.

After all this time...

who stayed with you?

Some memories
do not need words.
