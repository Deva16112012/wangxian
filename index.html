Some stories end.

...

And then,
somehow,

you keep returning to them.

Do you want to go back?

Then tell me—

where did you
first find them?

Memories blur with time.

What was this one called?

Ah.

You do remember.

You already know what happens.

Stay anyway.

Somewhere between the first note
and the last goodbye,

they stayed.

You remember
the sound of it.

Some expressions

never left.

It happened

like this.

01

Cloud Recesses.

Before either of them knew what would remain.

02

The cave.

A song, before its name meant everything.

03

Sunshot Campaign.

Things changed. They noticed.

04

Burial Mounds.

You already know this part.

05

Sixteen years.

And still, recognition.

06

The temple.

Some truths arrive very late.

Things they said.

Things you heard
differently the second time.

You remember

the sound.

After all this time...

who stayed with you?

Maybe you came back
because you missed them.

Maybe you missed
who you were when you found them.

Come back later.
